LarpManager is free and open source - no limits, no hidden fees, and no premium tiers. You can use it as a hosted service or run your own instance. This flexibility is at the heart of our mission: empowering LARP organizers of all sizes with powerful tools, without financial barriers.

Whether you're planning a small one-shot or managing a complex, persistent campaign, you have two ways to use the platform:

Use the hosted version

The easiest way to get started is by using our hosted platform here at larpmanager.com. It's ready to go - no setup required!

Content policy

We reserve the right to suspend or remove access to the hosted platform for events that include offensive, discriminatory, or otherwise harmful content. Let's keep LARP inclusive and respectful for everyone.

LARP is about storytelling, immersion, and community. We believe technology should support that - not restrict it.
